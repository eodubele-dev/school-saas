They are known for their rich history, sophisticated culture, and strong Islamic faith.</p><ul><li><p><strong>Origin:</strong> The origin of the Hausa people is often linked to the legendary Bayajidda story, which speaks of a prince from Baghdad who slew a serpent in Daura and married the Queen. These were traditionally divided into two categories:</p><p>Category States Description <strong>Hausa Bakwai (The Seven True Hausa States)</strong> Daura, Kano, Katsina, Zaria (Zazzau), Gobir, Rano, Biram These were considered the original or 'true' Hausa states, often founded by the direct descendants of the legendary Bayajidda.
